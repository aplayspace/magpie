## Inspiration & Vision
Drawing from Mary Ruefle's erasure poetry work in "A Little White Shadow", Magpie aims to democratize the art of erasure poetry, making it accessible to anyone with a mobile device. The tool bridges physical and digital creative practices, enabling users to discover new meanings through selective obscuring of text.
